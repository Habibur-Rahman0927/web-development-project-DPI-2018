Dhaka polytechnic institute CAMPUS

The main campus is a large four-storied building. A branch of the Sonali Bank has been established for the DPI students' banking facilities, which is located at the northern corner of the main building. A large auditorium also located just in the main campus for various events and cultural activities. The southern corner has a Shaheed Minar.

Dhaka polytechnic institute Workshop

A large well-equipped workshop is located on the main campus to enhance students' practical skills. Practical classes for Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Automobile & Refrigeration, Chemical, and Chemical (Food) are frequently held in the workshop.

Dhaka polytechnic institute Library

DPI library[7] has a collection of 20,000 books and 10,000+ bound periodicals. Besides, 37 titles are in the current subscription list of journals. The main reading room of central library can accommodate 100 students at a time to provide reading facilities of rare and out of print books, ready reference and prescribed textbooks.
