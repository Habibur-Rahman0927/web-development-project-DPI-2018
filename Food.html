Introduction to FOOD

Food technology is concerned with the process of getting food from the farm to the supermarket. Food technologists work in food research and development to ensure the quality and safety of food products. Continue reading to learn more about the ﬁeld of food technology. Inside Food Technology Food technology involves all aspects of food science, including production, processing and distribution. Professionals work to improve manufacturing methods through preservation, storage and new product development. They work with food scientists to enhance the nutritional value of foods.
